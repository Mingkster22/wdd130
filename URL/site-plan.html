FAQ

  • Why will people come to your site?

    People will come to my website for places to go for a vacation recommendation. For example, I would expect people to come to my website and want to look for places to go on vacations.

  • What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

    What vacation it is recommending. Why it is recommending the vacation place. If it has a good amount of information about the vacation place, so that they will know enough about the vacation place.

  • What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?

    Visitors will want to be able to get enough information from the website, be able to make reservations or buy tickets to enter the vacation place, and a lot more.

    Because of the great color choices that were made on the website.
